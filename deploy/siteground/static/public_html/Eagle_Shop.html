How It Works

  1. Import Products: Browse AliExpress and add products to your store with your profit margin
  2. Customer Orders: Customers purchase from your store at your price
  3. Auto Fulfillment: System automatically orders from AliExpress supplier
  4. Direct Shipping: Supplier ships directly to customer
  5. Keep Profit: You keep the difference between customer price and AliExpress cost

Real Example

Wireless Earbuds:

  • AliExpress Cost: $4.99

  • Your Price: $7.49

Common Questions

Do I need inventory?
No! Eagle Shop is a dropshipping platform. Suppliers ship directly to customers.

How much does it cost to start?
It's free! You only pay a small fee on orders when you upgrade. No setup fees, no monthly charges unless you choose a paid plan.

Can I customize it?
Absolutely! Full source code access. Customize colors, domain, branding, and business logic.

What if a customer returns an order?
You can create return policies and handle disputes through the dashboard.

Can I use real products from AliExpress?
Yes! With your API credentials, you can import real products, set your margins, and start selling immediately.
